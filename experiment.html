Instructions

For this experiment, you will be shown 2 types of menu: Marking Menu and Radial Menu.

There will be a total of 54 trials, 27 for each menu type. For each trial, a target item is given. Use the menu to select the target item. When you have made your selection, click on the ‘Next’ button to proceed to the next trial.

For the marking menu:

  1. Right click to bring up the menu
  2. Swipe (while holding down the right mouse click) to make your selection
  3. Release the right mouse click to confirm your selection

For the radial menu:

  1. Right click to bring up the menu
  2. Move the cursor to the selection
  3. Left click to confirm your selection

On completion of experiment, there will be an exit survey followed by a downloadable copy of your results
